Our Programs

YouthHack Competitions

We host high school technopreneurship competitions where students get a chance to learn more about technology, entrepreneurship, rapid prototyping, and more. Last July 2014, we had more than 100 High School students participate in the first ever YouthHack Manila.

Internship Program

During the summer, we train and connect high school students with startups in different field so that they can gain organizational experience, learn more about an industry, and use their skills in actual projects
